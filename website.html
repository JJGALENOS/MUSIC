Welcome to JJ Galenos Music

A non-profit Venezuelan organization dedicated to the promotion, performance and diffusion of Latin American music in all of its forms. Our mission is to bring the art of music to all areas of society and help bring people together from all stages in life.

About Us

Founded in 2018 by Dr. Jose Javier Blanco, an outstanding gynecologist with a passion for music and the arts, JJ Galenos Music strives to bring the art of music to all areas of society and to help bring people together from all stages in life.

Our mission as an organization is to showcase our Latin American roots and bring together people from all cultures, religions and communities in Canada. A journey that originally started in Venezuela a few years back turned into a dream and later became a reality when we opened our operations centre in Toronto on November 25, 2018.

Twice winner of the Venezuelan National Medical Voice Festival, Dr. Blanco has had a very illustrious musical career both in Venezuela and Canada. Over the years, he has been a member and musical director of various groups including the Carabobo School of Medicine Choir, the Venezuelan organization "Sentir Venezuela" and the prestigious "Upper Canada Choristers" and its "Cantemos" ensemble.

Whether in the operating room or the stage, Dr. Blanco plans to continue following his passions and use his talents to bring people together from all stages in life.
